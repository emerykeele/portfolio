Trading Cards

This project started in Spring 2019, and is still in progress. These are trading cards produced for the rapper Yung Gravy. I originally created the idea for these cards as well as the content in each one. The concepts behind each card comes from musical lyrics, as well as different personas he takes on in his music. I worked alongside him as well as his team for concepts as well as changes that had to be made. These are projected to be released as official merchandise under his name and sold at events as well as on his website. The results are still being finalized as to how the final contract will look, which will hopefully be within the next few months. (The second set card back is included for reference of the second set.)
